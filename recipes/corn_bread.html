Cornbread Recipe

Homepage

Description

Cornbread should be soft on the inside, slightly crisp on the edges, and lightly sweet without being cake-like. This recipe nails that balance. It’s quick, beginner-friendly, and uses ingredients you probably already have.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar (or adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk (whole or 2%)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup melted butter (or vegetable oil for a lighter texture)

Steps:

Prepare the pan and oven

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Grease an 8×8 baking dish or cast-iron skillet with butter or oil.

Mix the dry ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together:

  • Cornmeal
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Mix the wet ingredients

In a separate bowl whisk:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Melted butter (make sure it’s cooled slightly so it doesn’t scramble the eggs)

Combine wet and dry

  1. Pour the wet mixture into the dry.
  2. Gently stir until just combined — do not overmix.
  3. Batter should be slightly lumpy.

Bake

  1. Pour batter into your greased pan.
  2. Bake for 20–25 minutes or until:
    • The top is golden
    • A toothpick inserted comes out clean
  3. Let it cool for 10 minutes before cutting.
