Fried Chicken Recipe

Homepage

Description

Fried chicken is all about two things: flavorful seasoning and a shatter-crispy crust. This recipe uses a buttermilk soak to keep the meat tender and juicy, plus a seasoned flour mix that creates those beautiful crags and crispy bits on the outside. Perfect for dinner, weekend cooking, or impressing someone you care about.

Ingredients

For Chicken

  • 1 whole chicken cut into 8-10 pieces (or use thighs/wings/drumsticks)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • a teaspoon cayenne (optional for heat)

For Seasoned Flour

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch (extra crunch)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder (helps create a crisp airy crust)

For Frying

  • Oil for deep frying (peanut, canola, or vegetable)
  • Thermometer (optional but helpful)

Steps:

Marinate the chicken

  1. Add chicken pieces to a bowl.
  2. Pour in buttermilk, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne.
  3. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate at least 2 hours — ideally overnight for maximum juiciness.

Season the Flour

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together:
    • Flour
    • Cornstarch
    • All seasonings
    • Baking powder
  2. Taste the flour with a fingertip, should be salty and flavorful. Adjust seasoning if needed.

Dredge the Chicken

  1. Remove chicken from the buttermilk (don't wipe it off).
  2. Coat each piece thoroughly in the seasoned flour.
  3. Press the flour onto the chicken to form craggy bits.
  4. Shake off excess and place on a tray for 10 minutes so the coating adheres.

Heat the Oil

  1. Heat oil in a deep pot to 325-350°F (160-175°C).
  2. Avoid too hot — it burns the coating before the inside cooks.

Fry the Chicken

  1. Fry 3-4 pieces at a time without overcrowding.
  2. Maintain temperature around 325°F.
    • Wings: 8-10 minutes
    • Drumsticks: 12-14 minutes
    • Thighs/Breasts: 14-16 minutes
  3. Chicken should be golden, crispy, and reach an internal temp of 165°F (75°C).

Rest the Chicken

  1. Place the cooked chicken on a wire rack (not paper towels) for maximum crispiness.
  2. Let sit for 5-10 and enjoy!
